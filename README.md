🌱 GreenRoots Ventures

Climate-Smart Circular Farming & Clean Energy Solution

🌍 About GreenRoots
GreenRoots Ventures is a climate-focused social enterprise based in Lilongwe, Malawi. We transform agricultural waste such as maize cobs, rice husks, and groundnut shells into valuable products including biochar, organic fertilizer, and clean cooking briquettes.
Our work addresses key challenges in rural communities: deforestation, declining soil fertility, and limited income opportunities for youth and women.

⚠️ The Problem
Over 90% of households in Malawi rely on charcoal and firewood
This drives deforestation and environmental degradation
Farmers face poor soil fertility and high fertilizer costs
Agricultural waste is often burned or discarde

💡 Our Solution
We use a circular farming approach to turn waste into value:
Convert agricultural waste into biochar, briquettes, and organic fertilizer
Improve soil fertility and increase crop yields
Provide clean and affordable cooking fuel
Create income opportunities for youth and women

🔄 How It Works
Agricultural Waste → Processing → Biochar / Briquettes / Fertilizer → Farmers & Households

📊 Impact
Reduces deforestation and waste burning
Improves soil health and food production
Creates jobs for youth and women
Promotes climate resilience in rural communities

🎯 Vision
To build a scalable, community-driven system that transforms waste into opportunity, strengthens food security, and supports sustainable livelihoods across Malawi and beyond.

📍 Location
Lilongwe District, Malawi
🚀 Future Plans
Scale production of briquettes and biochar
Expand to more farming communities
